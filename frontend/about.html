About the Project

What are AI Hallucinations?

Hallucinations occur when a Large Language Model (LLM) generates text that is grammatically correct but factually false, nonsensical, or unfaithful to the provided source material.

Why are they dangerous?

In fields like medicine, law, or software engineering, a single hallucinated fact can lead to critical errors, security vulnerabilities, or misinformation spread at scale.

The TrustGuard Solution

TrustGuard AI uses an ensemble of verification techniques to cross-reference AI outputs against real-world data, ensuring that the information you use is grounded in reality.
